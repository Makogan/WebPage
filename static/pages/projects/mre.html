My honours thesis, conducted under Dr. Faramarz Samavati and with the Guidance of Troy Alderson at the University of Calgary. I studied how to extend Alderson's multi resolution scheme, which was suitable for spheres, to any arbitrary elliptical manifold.

By finding geometrical approaches to emulate spherical interpolation on ellipsoids the previous method could be adapted to new the topologies with no other modifications to the algorithm.

Ultimately we could, qualitatively, reduce or increase the number of points defining a closed curve in the surface of an ellipsoid while maintaining the general shape of the original curve.

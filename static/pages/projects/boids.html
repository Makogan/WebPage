Boid simulation built for Computer Animation (CPSC 589), taught by Dr. Przemyslaw Prusinkiewicz at the University of Calgary, as part on an assignment.

Each boid follows a small set of rules that determine its behaviour based on that of its neighbours. A boid will move in teh same direction as its neighbours in a medium range, move away from its neighbours in a small range and try to match the direction of its neighbours in a large range. The result is an emergent behaviour that mimics ow swarms, flocks of birds, schools of fish and other large animal congregations behave.
